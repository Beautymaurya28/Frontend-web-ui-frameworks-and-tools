  • little intro about Banarsh and my first experience:

    • Banaras, one of the world's oldest continuously inhabited cities, is a treasure trove of spirituality, culture, and history. As I stepped into this ancient city, I was immediately struck by its vibrant energy and timeless charm. My first experience in Banaras was a mesmerizing sunset boat ride along the Ganges River, watching the city come alive with twinkling diyas and chanting sadhus. As the boat glided past the ghats, I felt the cool river breeze carry the whispers of the past, while the setting sun painted the sky with hues of crimson and gold. The sweet scent of incense and fresh flowers filled the air, drawing me into the city's mystical embrace. It was a truly surreal experience, and I knew in that moment, Banaras had already left an indelible mark on my heart
